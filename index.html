HOW TO PLAY

The rule is simple: type the word or the sentence before it falls to the ground !

You will have 5 sec. to guess the words (EASY MODE)
10 sec. to guess the sentence (MEDIUM MODE)

For each word or sentence correct, you gain 50 points !

Don't do mistakes, it will remove you 100 points

Check out the ZEN mode for a chill experience
